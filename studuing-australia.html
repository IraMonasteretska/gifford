Studying in Australia

Study abroad in Australia at one of our Gifford Academy campuses in Sydney or the Gold Coast

Studying in Australia is a fantastic way to enjoy the exciting cities of Sydney and the Gold Coast while getting a world-class education

To study abroad in Australia, you need a student visa from the Australian government. You can choose an education agent to help you with the application process.

Before you arrive in Australia, you’ll need to arrange your Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC).
