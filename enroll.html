Enrolment Form

Please use the form below to enrol with Gifford Academy. To complete this form you will need a Unique Student Identifier (USI). This is a mandatory code the Australian Government requires for all education.
Your USI (Unique Student Identifier) is your individual education number for life. It also creates an online record of your Australian training achievements. To retrieve your USI or apply for a new one please visit usi.gov.au/students. The process should take less than 5 minutes. Note: If you are an overseas student, you do not require a USI.
