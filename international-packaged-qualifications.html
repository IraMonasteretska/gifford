Entry requirements for international students

To be eligible to study at Gifford Academy, you need:

  • to be at least 18 years old
  • to have a minimum of an Australian High School Certificate or have graduated from high school in your country
  • a reasonable amount of computer literacy in word processing, spreadsheets, email and internet browsing
  • sufficient proficiency in reading, writing, typing and speaking English.

You will need to provide evidence of your English language proficiency as this is a requirement for majority of our courses.
