University Pathways

After completing a Diploma with Gifford Academy, you'll be well on your way to a fulfilling university life and a rewarding career. Our Diploma courses can shave off up to a year of study in your chosen university pathway, paving the way to a degree in early childhood teaching or youth work-related studies. University articulation agreements and pathways are continually evolving, ensuring you have the most up-todate opportunities for academic and professional advancement.

What pathways do we have available?
