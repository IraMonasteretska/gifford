Get started on the career of your dreams

If you’re passionate about molding and educating the young minds of Australia’s youth, then you should contemplate enrolling in the CHC50121 Diploma of Early Childhood Education and Care. This program will equip you with the skills to plan, execute, and oversee programs in early childhood services while adhering to licensing, accreditation, and duty of care standards. With legislation mandating that most educators in the early childhood services industry possess or be working towards a Diploma level qualification, there’s no better time than now to pursue the CHC50121 Diploma of Early Childhood Education and Care. This course will provide you with the knowledge to plan, execute, and oversee programs in early childhood services.

Course Description

1

Course Requirement

In order to obtain the CHC50121 Diploma of Early Childhood Education and Care, you must successfully fulfill the requirements of 15 units of competency, consisting of 12 core units and 3 elective units.

Candidates are also expected to complete at least 280 working hours in an Approved Service, which will enable them to undertake various relevant tasks associated with the course.

2

Entry Requirements

Students will be required to complete and pass a Working With Vulnerable People Check (including a national police check) in the state or territory that they are working in prior to commencing work in this field.

Entry to this qualification is open to individuals who hold one of these previous qualifications:

CHC30121 – Certificate III in Early Childhood Education and Care

CHC30113 – Certificate III in Early Childhood Education and Care

3

Student Selection Process

GA’s student selection process is designed to ensure that you are enrolled in the qualification that best meets your needs.

GA’s selection criteria will take into account various factors when deciding upon which students will be offered places in the program including;

  • The students’ needs and desired outcomes
  • The ability and commitment of the students to complete the course;
  • Eligibility requirements listed in the applicable National Training Package;
  • Students existing ability in the ACSF core skills, (learning, reading, writing, oral communication and numeracy)

Students not meeting these requirements will be directed to more suitable qualifications or skill development programs.

Course Structure

To achieve the CHC50121 Diploma of Early Childhood Education and Care a total of 15 units of competency must be completed (12 core units and 3 elective units).

Core Units

The following 15 core units must be completed:

BSBTWK502

Manage team effectiveness

CHCECE041

Maintain a safe and healthy environment for children

CHCECE042

Foster holistic early childhood learning, development and wellbeing

CHCECE043

Nurture creativity in children

CHCECE044

Facilitate compliance in a children’s education and care service

CHCECE045

Foster positive and respectful interactions and behaviour in children

CHCECE046

Implement strategies for the inclusion of all children

CHCECE047

Analyse information to inform children’s learning

CHCECE048

Plan and implement children’s education and care curriculum

CHCECE049

Embed environmental responsibility in service operations

CHCECE050

Work in partnership with children’s families

CHCPRP003

Reflect on and improve own professional practice
